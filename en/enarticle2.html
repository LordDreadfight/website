My Laptop

My self-built laptop and its abrupt demise, from 06.10.2024

Hello, this post is about my DIY laptop that I built myself. As some of you may know, a power bank deflagrated in my room in June (Deflagration describes a combustion process that is too slow for an explosion and too fast for a regular fire). Everything that was on my desk was destroyed either by the flames or by the ABC powder from the fire extinguisher. This included my PC, 2 of my 3 monitors, and also my laptop. Here’s a picture: These are the remains of my laptop. The laptop burned down less than 2 weeks after completion. :c. This laptop was over 3 years in the making and cost me countless sleepless nights. I won’t talk about the actual price of the laptop right now, but it was in the 4-figure range, and it hurts just to think about it. This is what the laptop looked like when it was finished: The laptop was 100% DIY. It had an SBC with an N100 CPU, 16GB RAM, a 1TB SSD, and a gorgeous FHD 13.3-inch IPS display with colors so beautiful that OLED displays would be envious. Of course, the specs are weak for a laptop that cost me €1500, but I spent several hundred hours designing, printing, redesigning, soldering, and wiring the laptop. As I said, a how-to is coming soon, and to conclude, I’d like to share a few more pictures of the laptop.
